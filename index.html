Who is the CEO?
Mr. Munsif Lashari is the CEO of Max Green Skill Development Organization. He leads various educational initiatives and partnerships.
What is Non Formal Education?
Non-Formal Education (NFE) provides learning opportunities to out-of-school children and adults in marginalized communities through flexible programs.
Recent Event Details
Recent events include monitoring visits in Tharparkar, LoU signing for 133 NFE centers, and meetings with Sindh Education Foundation officials.
What courses are available?
We offer Non-Formal Education programs, Adult Literacy courses, Skill Development training, and Adolescent Learning programs.
How to apply?
You can apply through our local NFE centers, contact our regional offices, or visit our official websites for enrollment information.
What are the benefits?
Benefits include free education, skill development, community empowerment, employment opportunities, and personal growth.
What is the duration?
Course durations vary from 3 months for basic literacy to 1 year for comprehensive skill development programs.
Is there any fees?
Most programs are free of cost, especially Non-Formal Education initiatives supported by government partnerships.

Surprise Monitoring Visits ‚Äì Lighting the Path of Learning in Tharparkar üåü

In a continued effort to ensure quality education and impactful learning experiences, CEO Max Green Skill Development Organisation, Mr. Munsif Lashari, and District Education Officer Literacy (DEO) Mr Kastoor Chand along with Program Manager NFE, Mr. Hamza Khalid, conducted surprise monitoring visits to various Non-Formal Education (NFE) Centres across District Tharparkar.

The purpose of these visits was to review the progress of the centers, evaluate learner attendance, and observe community participation ‚Äî key elements that ensure sustainability and strengthen the spirit of local ownership in education initiatives.

During the visits, the team expressed satisfaction over the dedication of learners and facilitators while encouraging continuous improvement to make learning more engaging and effective for children and adults alike.

These NFE Centres are being operated under the management of Max Green Skill Development Organisation, with the valuable support of the Directorate of Literacy and Non-Formal Education, Government of Sindh.

Who is the CEO ?


Sir Munsif Ali Lashari is the Chief Executive Officer of Max Green Skills Development Organization. He has been leading the organization since 2010 with a vision to empower communities through education and skill development.



What is Non Formal Education ?


Non-Formal Education at Max Green is a flexible learning system designed for those who cannot access formal education. It focuses on practical skills, literacy, and vocational training tailored to community needs.



Recent Event Details ? 


CEO Sir Munsif Ali Lashari recently participated in the Letter of Understanding Signing and Opening Ceremony of 133 Non-Formal Education Centers at Beach Luxury Hotel, Karachi. This event celebrated a powerful partnership for advancing education.




What Courses Are Available ?


We offer various Non-Formal Education courses including: Basic Literacy & Numeracy, Vocational Training (tailoring, computer skills), Life Skills, Digital Literacy, Environmental Education, and Community Development programs.



How to Apply ?


To apply for Non-Formal Education, visit our center with your CNIC/B-Form, 2 passport photos, and basic educational documents (if any). Our team will guide you through the simple registration process.



What Are The Benefits ?


Benefits include: Flexible learning schedules, Practical skill development, No age restrictions, Community-based learning, Certificate upon completion, and Improved employment opportunities.



What is the durations ?


Program duration varies: Basic courses (3-6 months), Vocational training (6-12 months), Specialized skills (3-9 months). We offer flexible timing to accommodate your schedule.




Is there Any Fees ?



Most Non-Formal Education programs are FREE! We believe in accessible education for all. Some specialized vocational courses may have minimal material costs.

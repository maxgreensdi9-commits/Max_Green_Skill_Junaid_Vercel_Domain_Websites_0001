






Mukesh Kumar, teacher at MAX GREEN NFE Center Shive Colony UC Bhitaro Taluka Diplo Distt Tharparkar, conducted the monthly 
progress test and awarded positions to the top-performing students.
This activity is part of the Non-Formal Education (NFE) Program, which provides learning opportunities to out-of-school children 
and adults in marginalized communities.
Recognizing students’ achievements helps motivate them to continue learning and improving.
